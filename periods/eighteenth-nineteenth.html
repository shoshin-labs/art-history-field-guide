Overview

At a glance

This period is best read as a set of arguments rather than a single march of styles: pleasure contests virtue, dream contests reason, and ordinary labour enters the picture with new seriousness.

not one period but a crowded fieldpolitics enters visiblyordinary life becomes serious subject matter
