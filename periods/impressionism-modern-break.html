Impressionism to the modern break

Light loosens, brushwork shows, structure shifts, and painting starts to stop behaving like a window

Key question: How do you get from Monet to Picasso without it feeling like a cliff-edge?

What this page is for

Impressionism to the modern break in plain English

This is one of the most useful routes for your taste. Start with light and atmosphere, then move to modern life, then to Van Gogh’s feeling and Cézanne’s structure. By then Picasso makes much more sense.
