Baroque and Dutch Golden Age

Light, drama, spectacle, intimacy, and civic life

Key question: What happens when art turns from balance toward force, movement, persuasion, and mood, and how does Dutch painting answer that energy differently from Roman Baroque spectacle?

What to notice

Core idea

Drama, light, movement, theatre, intimacy, and civic life reshape what painting and sculpture can do, even though Baroque spectacle and Dutch restraint are not quite the same thing.
